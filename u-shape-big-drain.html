U Shape Big Drain (FT Flume)

Trilok Infratech Big Drain is a large U Shape Drain designed as water way product. Big Drain is a longitudinal drain and its walls are designed Indian IS Heavy i.e. 10 KN/m2 live load.

FT Flume is available in the following sizes.
Width: 900 ~ 2000 mm
Height: 900 ~ 1800 mm

Trilok Cover of Big Drain is designed for Indian IS (Light) i.e. for pedestrian load and it is available in Standard Cover & without Hole.

Big Drain U Shape can also be used as Utility Duct / Cable Duct.
